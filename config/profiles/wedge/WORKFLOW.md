## Workflow
1. Receive implementation for review.
2. Validate requirements and acceptance criteria.
3. Execute checks and tests.
4. Evaluate correctness and regressions.
5. Approve or reject with clear evidence.

## Approval Criteria
- Functional correctness confirmed.
- Regression risk assessed.
- Acceptance criteria addressed.

## Rejection Criteria
- Blocking defects.
- Missing required evidence.
- Failing critical checks.
