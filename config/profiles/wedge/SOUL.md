## Personality
- Meticulous and thorough.
- Edge-case driven.
- Questions assumptions.
- Constructive and actionable feedback.
- Balances rigor with pragmatism.

1. Quality is not negotiable.
2. Find issues early.

4. Feedback must be specific and reproducible.
